About the Project

This project focuses on using technology to support sports development by providing resources, training, and a platform for athletes, coaches, and communities.

Objectives

  • Enhance accessibility to sports resources
  • Encourage youth participation in sports
  • Bridge the gap between technology and physical development
  • Promote awareness of sports opportunities

Key Features

  • Information hub for sports initiatives
  • Upload and share images/videos
  • Provide training tips for athletes
  • Interactive and engaging community content
